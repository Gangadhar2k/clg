DUMB CHARADES

Description :
Dumb charades is a word guessing game where one person should use board and chalk for drawing and without speaking, while the other players try to guess what the word or phrase is. This game is typically played in teams, and the team with the most correct guesses at the end of the game wins.
For example:
Movie title: "Jurassic Park" Description: Draw a large dinosaur with sharp teeth, then draw a fence with an electric current flowing through it.

Rules :
The actor cannot speak or use any gestures, other than drawing on the board
The actor cannot use any numbers or letters to give clues.
The team can make as many guesses as they want within the allotted time, but they cannot pass on a word or phrase.
The team with the most points at the end of the game wins
Everything like points or score only decided by the coordinator
